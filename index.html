May 2022 - Jul 2022                       github

Poultry Feed Efficiency System

We have developed a mobile application in collaboration with the faculty of Agriculture at the University of Peradeniya. The app is designed to assist users in recording and managing poultry feeding details on a daily basis. It allows users to track various parameters such as feed amount, body weight, mortality count, egg count, and FCR (Feed Conversion Ratio) value. The application also includes an analytics view, providing users with an overview of the overall condition of the chickens. To develop the app, we utilized Flutter and Dart, and Firebase Services were employed for the backend management.

Jan 2022 - Mar 2022                       github

Student Results Management System

This mobile application is designed for undergraduate students to manage and analyze their marks. It eliminates the need for supervisors or lecturers as the students themselves can utilize it. The application allows batch administrators and field administrators to register courses with relevant details. Students can then enter their final confirmed marks or grades, which are securely saved without being visible to others. Additionally, students can calculate their current GPA and set their expected GPA for future semesters. The system can provide necessary grades required in upcoming semesters to achieve the expected GPA. For courses with continuous assessment, students can determine the required exam marks needed to attain their desired grade. The application also provides the student's rank within the batch and field based on the confirmed marks entered. The application is developed using Android Studio.
